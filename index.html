Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your data is collected, used, and safeguarded when you utilize the automation integrations, applications, and workflow pipelines (the "Service") operated under BritSync ("we", "us", or "our") connecting with third-party networks, including but not limited to TikTok and Meta Graph APIs.

1. Information We Collect

To facilitate automated content scheduling, rendering, and publishing from your storage directories (such as Google Drive) directly to your social media channels, our Service requests access to specific information:

  • User Information: Basic profile identifiers, handles, account nicknames, and user identification tokens provided during secure OAuth2 authorization steps.
  • Media and Content Assets: Public video assets, image binaries, text captions, and corresponding metadata explicitly targeted by the user for media distribution execution workflows.
  • System Log Data: Processing transaction details, webhook response codes, timestamp sequences, and diagnostic server data.

2. How We Use Data

We utilize the collected information strictly under the principle of data minimization to execute direct media management commands requested by the user:

  • To authenticate API sessions via secure tokens (user.info.basic).
  • To stream designated video binaries directly into platform staging caches (video.upload).
  • To officially schedule or publish finished video distributions to feed streams or user profile folders (video.publish).

3. Data Retention and Safeguards

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, distribute, trade, or share any personal identity indicators or raw asset files with external third-party data brokers.
  • Token Security: All access tokens, authentication keys, and client_secret parameters are heavily encrypted in-transit (TLS 1.2+) and at-rest, restricted strictly on a "need-to-know" administrative access basis.
  • Retention Period: We do not maintain or store user media assets on our infrastructure beyond the processing duration required to finalize transmission to the targeted social media networks.

4. Data Subject Rights

Users maintain complete, unmitigated ownership of their social data records. You retain the absolute right to terminate data processing linkages or request full token/data deletion across our connected frameworks at any time by contacting our developer administration desk at britsyncuk@gmail.com.
